Who We Are

Inspiring the Next Generation of Space Explorers.

India’s first dedicated Analog and Real-Time Astronaut Training Center, pioneering space simulation environments and mission readiness programs. At the intersection of innovation, science, and education, we empower future astronauts, researchers, and explorers with world-class training and experiential learning.

What We Do

Where Earth Meets Space Exploration

We are a passionate team of aerospace engineers, analog astronauts, and STEM educators united by one mission—preparing India and the world for the new space age. Our facility replicates extraterrestrial environments with cutting-edge analog simulation, enabling experiential astronaut training, scientific research, and psychological studies under mission-realistic conditions.

Whether it's a simulated Lunar base, Martian habitat, or microgravity conditions—our programs are crafted to challenge, engage, and transform individuals and teams into space-ready professionals.

Founded with a vision to place India at the forefront of space mission readiness and human spaceflight research, our center is not just a training facility—it's a launchpad for dreams.
